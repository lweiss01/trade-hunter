Minimum raw volume change to consider a spike. Lower = more sensitive.

Score threshold
Minimum combined score (volume + price) to fire an alert.

SPIKE_COOLDOWN_SECONDS · default 300
Signal tiers (read-only)
Watch

Notable
Price move ≥ 1.2× min and volume ≥ 2.4× baseline, or score ≥ 4.0.
High conviction flow
Score ≥ 6, price move ≥ 1.75× min, volume ≥ 3× baseline simultaneously.
Cross-venue divergence

Applied immediately — no restart needed.

Advisor snapshot A · 2026-04-03 Detector flagging massive volume spikes with zero price movement — order-book churn, not conviction flow. Nearly all false positives share priceΔ = 0.0.
TB-001
applied
Add minimum price-move gate for notable alerts.
Rule: reject notable promotion unless |priceΔ| ≥ 0.5%.
Implemented in detector.py via notable_min_price_move = 0.005 inside _tier(). Covered in test_detector_migration.py.
TB-002
planned
Extend minimum price-move gate to watch alerts.
Rule: reject watch alerts unless |priceΔ| ≥ 0.5% for liquid markets.
Advisor recommends this apply to both watch and notable. Not implemented yet. Overlaps with TB-006.
TB-003
applied
Stricter gating for illiquid or ultra-low-price markets.
Rule: require |priceΔ| ≥ 2% when yes_price ≤ 0.01 or liquidity ≤ 1000.
Implemented via _required_price_move() in detector.py. Covered in test_detector_migration.py.
TB-004
applied
Ultra-thin market volume rule.
Rule: if volΔ < 500 or yes_price ≤ 0.01, require 100×+ volume multiplier or executed trades > 0 before alerting.
Implemented via _ultra_thin_market_ok(). Quote-only ultra-thin spikes below 100× baseline are suppressed. Covered in test_detector_migration.py.
TB-005
applied
Order-flow coherence check.
Rule: reject alerts where side-specific trade flow and price direction disagree, or where balanced/quote-only weak-price volume spikes lack confirming flow.
Implemented via _trade_flow_is_coherent() plus existing directional-flow gating. Covered in test_detector_migration.py.
Advisor snapshot B · 2026-04-03 Watch-tier alerts firing on volume spikes without price conviction or directional trade confirmation — noise in balanced or low-momentum markets.
TB-006
planned
Higher watch-tier minimum move when volume is weak relative to baseline.
Rule: for watch, require 1–2% price move when volΔ < 2× baseline; require 5–6% if volΔ is only 1.2× baseline.
Overlaps with TB-002, may supersede it once implemented.
TB-007
applied
Directional trade-flow confirmation.
Rule: require >60% of recent trades to lean one side before escalating balanced volume spikes.
Implemented in detector.py. Weak-price outlier alerts require either meaningful repricing (≥ 1%) or directional confirmation (>60% dominant trade-side). Covered in test_detector_migration.py.
TB-008
planned
